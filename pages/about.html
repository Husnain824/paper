About IlmAI

IlmAI is a free, AI-powered study companion built for Pakistani students in Class 9 through 12.

Our Mission

We believe every student in Pakistan deserves access to high-quality, curriculum-aligned study tools — regardless of which board they belong to or where they live. IlmAI uses AI to generate practice MCQs, check answers, summarize chapters, and build full board-pattern test papers, all tailored to Pakistan's actual syllabus.

What We Offer

  • AI-generated MCQs for every subject, class, and board
  • Instant AI answer checking for short and long questions
  • Roman Urdu and English AI chat tutor, available anytime
  • Image scanner (OCR) for textbook pages and handwritten notes
  • AI chapter summaries and board-pattern guess papers
  • Full test mode — complete board-pattern or custom exam papers

Boards Covered

Punjab, Federal, Lahore, Rawalpindi, Faisalabad, Gujranwala, Multan, Sargodha, KPK, Karachi, Hyderabad, Balochistan, AJK, and Gilgit-Baltistan boards.
