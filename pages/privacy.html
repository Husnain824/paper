Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

What We Store

IlmAI stores your study progress (streak, XP), quiz history, and preferences locally in your browser using localStorage. This data never leaves your device except when sent to our AI provider to generate study content.

AI Processing

When you request MCQs, summaries, or ask the AI tutor a question, your text (and optionally an uploaded image for scanning) is sent to our AI provider (Groq or Google Gemini) to generate a response. We do not store this content on our servers beyond what's needed to serve the request.

Third-Party Services

IlmAI uses Groq and Google Gemini for AI responses, and OCR.space for text extraction from images. These providers process data according to their own privacy policies.
